AskMeApp is a tool for communication between streamers and viewers.

AskMeApp allows streamers to receive questions from viewers and provide viewers with feedback in the form of private messages or timestamps when they have answered a viewer's question during a broadcast.




  • Register as a Streamer
  • Add a chat for your streaming broadcast
  • Save and edit your streaming information
  • Receive questions from your viewers
  • Save answers as private messages in the chat
  • Time-stamp your answers in the chat when you give answers during the broadcast
  • Register as a viewer
  • Select streaming and register in the chat
  • Send questions to the host via chat
  • Receive personal answers in the chat or information about the response time, to easily find the answer to your question in the streaming recording
